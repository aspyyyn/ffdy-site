Volunteer With FFDY

Be part of something meaningful. Our volunteers are the heartbeat of everything we do — and we'd love to have you join us.

Why It Matters

Your Time Makes All the Difference

FFDY operates thanks to the dedication of over 60 volunteers who show up each week to run programs, support participants, and bring genuine joy to our community.

Volunteers come from all walks of life — students, professionals, retirees, and everyone in between. What unites them is a shared belief that every person deserves to be seen, supported, and celebrated.

How You Can Help

Volunteer Opportunities

We welcome volunteers for a variety of roles during our weekly sessions. No special experience is needed — just a warm heart and a willingness to engage.

🎨

Arts & Crafts

Help participants explore creative expression through painting, drawing, and mixed media projects.

🎵

Music & Movement

Lead or assist with singing, rhythm exercises, and light instrument activities.

🎲

Board Games & Social Activities

Facilitate group games, conversation, and social engagement during leisure time.

🏃

Adaptive Sports

Support physical activity and movement-based programs with patience and encouragement.

🍎

Snack Preparation

Help prepare and serve healthy snacks during program breaks — always a popular part of the day.

🌟

Event Support

Assist with birthday celebrations, holiday events, field trips, and our annual appreciation gala.

🏅

Presidential Volunteer Award

High school students who complete 100 volunteer hours within one year at FFDY may qualify for the Presidential Volunteer Service Award — a nationally recognized honor for outstanding community service.

Volunteering with FFDY also fulfills many high schools' community service hour requirements. We provide signed documentation for your records.

For Students

Give Back. Earn Recognition.

FFDY is a perfect volunteer destination for high school and college students looking to make a real impact, build their resumes, and fulfill community service requirements.

Our student volunteers tell us that their time at FFDY often becomes one of the most meaningful experiences of their academic years — building empathy, leadership, and lifelong connections.
