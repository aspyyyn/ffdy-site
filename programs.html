What We Offer

Programs & Activities

Weekly classes and events designed to build skills, spark joy, and foster a sense of belonging for individuals with I/DD.

🎨

Art Class

Our art program is a creative sanctuary where imagination leads the way. Participants work with paint, collage, clay, and mixed media — developing fine motor skills, focus, and self-confidence along the way.

Every piece created is celebrated, because we believe in the power of artistic expression to communicate what words sometimes cannot.

Weekly Sessions
All Skill Levels
🎵

Music Class

Music is a universal language, and our class harnesses that power. Participants enjoy singing, rhythm exercises, and introductory instrument exploration in a fun, structured environment.

Music class builds confidence, coordination, and social connection — and it brings out smiles every single week.

Weekly Sessions
All Ages
🥋

Taekwondo

Our adaptive taekwondo program is structured specifically for individuals with I/DD. Participants build physical coordination, balance, discipline, and — most importantly — a sense of personal accomplishment.

With patient instructors and an encouraging group environment, every participant progresses at their own pace and celebrates real milestones.

Adaptive Format
Beginner Friendly
👥

Adult Program

Our adult-focused programming addresses the unique needs of grown members of the I/DD community. Sessions cover life skills, social engagement, job readiness exercises, and recreational activities tailored for adults.

We believe adult members deserve rich programming, not just childcare — and our program reflects that commitment.

Adults Only
Life Skills Focus
Beyond the Classroom

Special Activities & Events

Throughout the year, FFDY hosts memorable experiences that go beyond weekly classes — creating lasting memories and expanding horizons.

🐴

Horseback Riding

Therapeutic equine activities that build sensory awareness, balance, and emotional confidence in a beautiful outdoor setting.

🐾

Animal Therapy

Structured sessions with therapy animals that reduce anxiety, improve communication, and bring immense joy to participants.

🍳

Cooking Classes

Hands-on culinary sessions that teach real-world kitchen skills, encourage creativity, and produce delicious results.

🎡

Recreation Trips

Group outings to Disneyland, Sea World, local aquariums, and other destinations — making unforgettable memories together.

🎂

Birthday Celebrations

Monthly birthday parties where every member feels celebrated and recognized as a valued part of the FFDY family.

🏆

Annual Appreciation Gala

A special yearly event recognizing volunteers, supporters, and community partners who make our programs possible.
